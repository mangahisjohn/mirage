Our vision

Mirage is an augmented reality platform designed to enhance all aspects of real life. We aim to work with companies to unify the many dispersed applications out there and provide consumers with a one-stop-shop for all their AR needs.

Augmented reality has a problem

Currently, the transformative power of AR remains largely inaccessible to consumers except through specific company apps designed for specific purposes. Consumers do not have easy access to augmented reality (AR) for entertainment and communication and companies are struggling to reach and retain users through the medium AR because of the way that these business-bespoke apps are dispersed.
